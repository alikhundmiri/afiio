What is Afiio?

It is your business card, online.

The Bio of your social media profile like twitter and reddit, is sometimes not enough to hold all the awesome things you've built.
A point comes where you have to choose which 3 or 4 projects you will mention on your bio description.
With the help of Afiio, you don't have to choose which of your work you want to showcase. You can post all your projects' details here, and paste your account's link on your social media description.

Where is Afiio useful?

  • When you are trying to get new clients, and you want to show then your previous websites.

  • When you have a website running successfully, and now you want to divert this traffic to your other website

  • When you are a solo bootstrapper!

Why is this not free?

Because everything that's free, doesn't last.

I want my services to last as long as possible. And also this being my fulltime job, I need to monetise my websites. The alternative to advertisment is payment from users.
Besides I am not asking much. Just to the amount sufficient enough to keep this service alive, without disturbing your experience of this website.
